How cold calls are scored here
We only hear you — not the prospect. So Claude scores your technique and setup, not what the prospect said. The question is: did you create the conditions for a real conversation?
Situation Did you show you know their world before pitching?
Pain Did you name a specific pain and create space for them to confirm it?
Impact Did you connect that pain to a real business consequence?
Right Account Did you show why this company specifically is a fit?
Right Persona Did you speak to their role and priorities, not a generic pitch?
